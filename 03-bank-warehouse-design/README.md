# Bank Warehouse Design

*A bank analytics modernization project covering dimensional modeling, data governance, and ETL operations.*

---

## The problem

The bank needed a stable analytics foundation for customer, product, and transaction reporting. Legacy operational systems were preventing consistent reporting, increasing manual rework, and delaying quarterly insights.

## The data

A bank data model designed for deposits, loans, branches, transactions, and risk attributes. The documents cover enterprise-grade dimensional design, data dictionary standards, and ETL runbook operations.

## The three documents

1. **Dimensional Model Design** — star schema design, fact and dimension tables, grain, and history management. 18 pages.
2. **Data Dictionary** — field definitions, source lineage, data types, and business rules. 14 pages.
3. **ETL Operations Runbook** — batch orchestration, data quality checks, recovery procedures, and deployment notes. 10 pages.

## What's surprising in the findings

The highest-value deliverable was a compact operational runbook that reduced deployment risk and gave developers a clear recovery path. A solid dimensional model made the analytics layer repeatable and easier to maintain.
